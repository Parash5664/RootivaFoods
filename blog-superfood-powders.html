Superfood powders: concentrated nutrition, simple to use

Superfood powders such as beetroot and amla deliver vitamins, minerals and phytonutrients in a convenient form. They’re perfect for boosting smoothies, porridge, soups and snacks without changing your routine.

Benefits at a glance

  • High nutrient density: concentrated sources of antioxidants and vitamins per small scoop.
  • Easy to add: blends smoothly into drinks and recipes, no chopping or peeling required.
  • Low wastage: long shelf life and portion control for consistent daily use.

How to use superfood powders

Use 1–2 teaspoons per serving for most powders. Start with smaller amounts for strong flavours (e.g., beetroot) and increase to taste.

  • Smoothies: 1 tsp beetroot or amla, banana, yoghurt or plant milk, and a handful of spinach.
  • Oats & porridge: stir 1 tsp into cooked oats with honey and seeds.
  • Soups & sauces: add 1 tsp to soups for extra colour and nutrition—adjust salt as needed.

Simple recipe: Beetroot power smoothie (serves 1)

1 small banana, 150ml milk or plant milk, 1 tsp beetroot powder, 1 tbsp oats, 1 tsp honey — blend until smooth. Beetroot adds natural sweetness, colour and nitrates that support circulation.

Choosing the right powder

Look for 100% single-ingredient powders without fillers. Check the colour and smell — they should be natural and vibrant. Store airtight, away from heat and light.
